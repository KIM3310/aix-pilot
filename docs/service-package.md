# Service Package - aix-pilot

This package defines the most practical service shape for this repository. It is public-safe: no price promises, financial projections, or buyer-specific contract assumptions are published here.

## Buyer and Wedge

- **Service lane:** Enterprise GenAI adoption
- **Primary buyer:** AI, data, security, and platform leaders validating governed GenAI rollout
- **Front-door offer:** Governed GenAI adoption sprint
- **Service outcome:** Turn RAG, evals, DLP, and approval gates into one buyer-reviewable rollout workflow.

## Build and Polish Priorities

- Tighten first-screen KPI and risk hierarchy
- Make eval and DLP gates visible in the buyer path
- Prepare calendar/intake handoff without public financial claims

## Buyer-Ready Deliverables

- Readiness map
- Data-boundary review
- Pilot acceptance criteria

## Activation Path

1. Use the public demo, README, screenshots, and tests to establish proof.
2. Confirm the buyer workflow, data boundary, owner, and acceptance criteria.
3. Build only the smallest workflow needed to prove the service outcome.
4. Add cloud resources, accounts, storage, or paid integrations only after the workflow boundary is approved.

## Margin and Risk Controls

- Reuse governance templates and enable model spend caps before buyer testing.
- Keep the first service scope narrow, repeatable, and evidence-led.
- Keep private financial planning outside the public repository.
- Keep logs, analytics, and reports free of private payloads unless explicit approval exists.

## Portfolio Signal

The portfolio should present this repository as **Governed GenAI adoption sprint** under the **Enterprise GenAI adoption** lane, with proof links routed to the live demo or repository evidence.
