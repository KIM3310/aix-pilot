# Monetization Playbook - aix-pilot

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2B enterprise GenAI adoption platform
- **Primary buyer:** Customer support, internal knowledge, IT governance, and AI adoption leaders.
- **Revenue wedge:** Free-first RAG/Agent/security/KPI pilot that converts into governed deployment revenue.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Free proof-of-value demo** - pricing hypothesis: Free demo -> $5k setup
2. **Secure knowledge onboarding setup** - pricing hypothesis: $15k-$50k pilot
3. **Monthly KPI and governance reporting** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$15k/month support

## Acquisition Channels

- Founder-led enterprise outreach
- Before/after support workflow demo
- Korean customer-center case study

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- npm run qa passes
- Live Cloudflare demo works
- Evaluation lab and DLP posture visible

## Risk Boundaries

- Customer data needs approved storage
- AI drafts require review
- No unsupported automation claims

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Run 10 target-account demos
- **60 days:** Package 6-week rollout
- **90 days:** Add buyer-specific ROI calculator screenshots

## Metrics That Matter

- Deflection rate
- Citation coverage
- Time saved per ticket

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
